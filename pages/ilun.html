SYNOPSIS

Jace Akeris is a commander under the federation's army who has to bring a rebel world to heel. He's served with vigor for most of his young life into his early twenties. With his squad of angels (mechs with metal wings) they take the fight to the surface of the anarchist's world. But what he finds there, in secret communication with rebel leaders, will change his view of the federation forever.
