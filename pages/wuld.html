BOOK ONE
BEING REWRITTEN

A Rogue's Tale

The Reach Of The Gods
SYNOPSIS

A thief with a past that haunted her was a tale as old as time, but Bee didn't give enough of a damn to tell it. Living in the city of Caliurn had been a challenge since she arrived strayed without a home at a young age, but she'd joined the thieves guild and made ends meet. Most of her days were taken up by taking some intricacy or precious jewel until Hathric Gayli showed up on her doorstep. He'd made her a deal. Steal a priceless vial from the king's chamber, or die.

She'd told her friends and the guild master and planned a betrayal. Now she just had to live long enough to see it through.

BOOK TWO
FIRST DRAFT COMPLETE

Aetherbound

The Reach Of The Gods
SYNOPSIS

Gods shouldn't lie. But some apparently do.

Faced with a new prophecy and the prospect of her father possibly being alive, Bee's life had turned on a heel after Hathric's appearance in it. With new friends and new information she presses onward to do her own research into the prophecy of the third great war — though she is skeptical. Bee must weigh if the gods have lied to her, or if they're telling the truth in this daring adventure of friends and monsters.

BOOK THREE
FIRST DRAFT IN PROGRESS

Twin Flames Of Wuld

The Reach Of The Gods
SYNOPSIS

Controlling primordial forces of the world is a bit harder than it seems.

Two brothers, Arden and Emrys, attend the Glyphwrite Academy in Jerektal — each bearing their respective runes of Fire and Life. What starts out as a simple four-year schooling turns grave. Between their harsh trials with the teachers at Glyphwrite and the ominous warnings being left near them, they must make a choice: continue on for the knowledge of their rune paths, or go home for safety.
