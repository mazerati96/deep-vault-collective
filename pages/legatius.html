The One I Forgot

Legatius
SYNOPSIS

Vex had a mechanical god living in his head, and he couldn't remember his life.

Vex had awoken one day among the barren plains of some ruined city only to find that his hands were scarred and his body torn in places that ached worse than anything imaginable. Upon probing he noticed a second voice in his head guiding him forward.

With an odd sense of dissociation, Vex has to try to navigate a world he's entirely forgotten with an entity he has no memory of ever being there, or any memory for that matter.
