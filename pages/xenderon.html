SYNOPSIS

No one explained your choices altered the magnitude of your soul — Vail had to find out for herself.

Vail wakes on a hellscape. She'd been killed in one of the world wars of her home as she fought against a tyrant. But for some reason, she'd ended up in the most desolate and hellish place she'd ever seen.

Navigating through monstrous creatures, other foul souls, and dangers beyond her imagining, Vail has to try to survive in the world of Xenderon.
