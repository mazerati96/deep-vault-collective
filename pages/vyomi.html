The Garden Of Stories

Vyomi
SYNOPSIS

The gardener had been taking care of souls for eons, until now.

Kalay had kept the paradise planet of Vyomi in good shape for as long as her existence permitted. But then a causal shift happened. The pillars of chaos and order had blinked out and vanished from the system — throwing the polarity balance into disarray.

Now Kalay must work to keep the paradise planet alive and thriving. Will the magnitude of the universe fall to the tilt?
