The Yokai Of Senjan

Rexus
SYNOPSIS

There was a demon that killed men living at the top of a mountain in his hometown.

Reku is a simple farm boy who lives in the village of Isu. Nothing was entirely remarkable about Isu except for its strange yearly tradition. They would send one young man up the mountain to try to slay the demon at the top that haunted their land. No one ever came back down.

When Reku is chosen to go up he is sure that these few weeks will be his last. Reku must climb the mountain with his supplies and slay the demon that has killed a hundred score men — but what he finds at the top is not what he expected.
