The Shaw Saga
SYNOPSIS

Murder on the rooftops.

In a small town called Wayward, a courier and private investigator named James Shaw uncovers a killing of one of his customers on his route with the symbol of the Inkrunners splayed across their chest. Struck by the gruesome work, Quadrant Officers are called, but James isn't satisfied. Something more has gone on behind the scenes rather than just a hit taken out on one of his regulars. Someone had framed the Inkrunners to brew the fire boiling beneath Wayward's surface.

As he prepares to leave his route for some weeks to pursue the killer, Reyes Inc and the Inkrunners close in on his independent town. With a faction war of freedom fighters and corp soldiers, can James figure out the nature of the killing before either side turns his streets into a bloodbath?

SYNOPSIS

Greed is the power that pulls all men below even the poorest of mobs.

Grayson Bennet is heir to one of the most powerful families in Viridian City. And he doesn't want it. He mostly spends his days lounging and taking advantage of his family's money, that is until one day when Reyes Inc HQ is bombed by the Inkrunners.

In a flash Grayson makes it out alive, but now has to fight against corporate politics against his name while learning about the mysterious group called the Inkrunners: freedom fighters that want to bring their color back to the monochrome world of Astraea.
