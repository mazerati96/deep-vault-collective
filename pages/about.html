About the Author

Johnathan Kemp is a fantasy author and world-builder who transports readers to fantastical realms inspired by his five-year-long Dungeons and Dragons campaign. Through the Deep Vault Collective, he creates interconnected stories across multiple worlds in The Firmament of Vel—a vast multiverse of epic adventures, intricate lore, and unforgettable characters.

The Journey Begins

What started as a tabletop RPG campaign evolved into a rich tapestry of narratives spanning countless worlds. From the elven rogue Bee Rael's adventures on Wuld to the mysterious tales of The Tale Devourer's Grimoire, each story is a gateway into the Deep Vault—a collection of universes waiting to be explored.
