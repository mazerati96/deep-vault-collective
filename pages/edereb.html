The Sins Of Dimitri Kamor

Edereb
SYNOPSIS

The sun had vanished years ago, leaving the world in an almost eternal darkness.

Dimitri is a lord's son on a mission to protect one of the last bastions of humanity from a horde of blood-drinking fiends and other monstrous creatures. He's had to do horrid things to even survive the barren land, but survive he has.

When Dimitri is faced with a choice to possibly save the world by tainting himself with the dark he fights, he must make an important choice. Succumb and bring light back to the world, or die in a quiet fire.
